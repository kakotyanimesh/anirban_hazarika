This is our Legacy

I live for adventure. That means whatever you have envisioned for your wedding, pre-wedding or engagement - I am your man. I am here to turn your love story into art. Whatever feels genuine to you feels genuine to me and I would be beyond honoured to join you in the journey to capture your story as honestly and authentically as I can!

Casey & Nick

Isle of Skye, September 2023

Casey & Nick chose to Elope, just the two of them, on the Isle of Skye. They made and registered their own tartan, drank whisky from the Quaich and celebrated their story in a way that was so tied into who they are. It was so incredibly special in every single way and we feel honoured to have been a part of it!
